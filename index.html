DIGANTE for your twilight years

DIGANTE is a premium category state of art seniors' living facility in the vast greenery of  a 60,000 sq.ft MANGO ORCHARD a 16,000 sq.ft  G+3 building  stands with all modern amenities. It’s situated in Chuchura the district head quarter of Hooghly, near Delhi Road, only 40 min. drive from Kolkata. This state of art OAH is the first time in Eastern India in it’s category at a very affordable cost. It's a "NO PROFIT NO LOSS"  venture of  BOSDU Charitable trust & an initiative of some passionate highly qualified senior doctors of Kolkata.

  • Build, run and actively managed by highly passionate senior doctors
  • Amidst of pollution free, vast mango orchard, natural lake, vegetable farming, park, jogging track, cycle lane, 24x7 reliable security system
  • Rooms are fully furnished AC/geyser, attached bath and modern western fittings, anti skidding tiles, Grab railings and much more
  • Independent/ Semi-assisted/ assisted living facility by a skilled team
  • Assistance in banking work, life certificate, IT return file, any pension related or legal services
  • Arranging regular video calls with family members staying far away
  • Nutritious, customized, fresh veg and non-veg balanced diet. Separate menu for all 7 days of the week
  • Special attention for Diabetes, Hypertension, Cardiac problem, Dementia, Alzheimer, Kidney disease, Dialysis .... and many more
  • Weekly (if needed daily) free inhouse health checkup. Emergency BP, Sugar, ECG, Oxygen facility. Arranging outside investigation, doctor visit, dialysis, emergency ambulance service

A Note from the Founders

Yes…… we can see the hopes & dreams of dignified aging in the eyes of our seniors.

Since long back we personally and some couple of like minded friends are involved in different charitable work in the society under the banner of Bengal Organisation Of Social Development & Upliftment (BOSDU), a Charitable Trust. Our main working arena was child and adult education, health related sectors of elderly people , mother & child health etc. With the marvel of modern nutrition, healthcare, education & sanitation life expectancy of people is rising gradually. So the community is having more people who are called “senior Citizens”. Modern life is hectic, jet set. People are travelling far & wide in search of jobs, educations & assignments. With children moving out of their own home & relocating or loss of their spouse, elders are often bound to be left alone at homes. If you think it’s a problem – life will be miserable, but if you think it’s the right time to start your own new life – it will add a new flavour & open a new horizon in your retired life.

During our visit to different parts of the city and around we noticed that senior citizens are leading a very very lonely, monotonous and distressing life, difficult to survive with dignity. Like ‘ Roti-Kapda our Makan’, nowadays special, trained, organized and affectionate care giver for the seniors are becoming the new wide spread demand of the vast senior community worldwide. From this idea to provide a systematic, regular, comprehensive, organized, supervised and efficient quality care to the seniors, DIGANTE came into being….. Built, Governed and Run by well established & Senior Doctors of Kolkata & Chuchura. In DIGANTE our endeavour is to give the senior residents utmost care, comfort, security, assistance, companionship, in house proper medical facilities and to take care of their other health and social needs. To ensure - physical, social and mental well being of our residents, is our topmost priority. Here everyone can spend their twilight years free from all worldly troubles and worries. Thus we promise to make DIGANTE truly a home away from own home.

Previously the idea of moving to old age homes were not so easily acceptable. It was difficult to accept the thought of leaving ones own home with it’s familiar environment, social contact & memories. ….but nowadays the concept has been changed , people are moving to old age homes by their own choice, as todays’ “senior Citizens” homes like ‘DIGANTE’ are very efficiently managed by highly qualified professionals in all aspects maintaining a homely atmosphere in all respects.
