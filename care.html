About This Venture

For over eight years, "DIGANTE Old Age Home" in Chuchura, has established itself as a beacon of trust, dignity, and compassionate care. Initially a residence, it evolved into a reassuring presence for families and a true home for seniors.

Over time, one heartfelt wish was voiced repeatedly: to receive the same quality of care at home. In response, we now offer premium, dignified, and affordable home care services, without compromising on warmth or quality at your door step.

Our organization is owned and managed by highly qualified senior doctors, whose extensive clinical experience shapes every aspect of care. Their leadership ensures medical integrity, ethical practice, and empathetic decision-making. This doctor-led model blends professional excellence with emotional sensitivity.

Our Mission

To enhance the quality of life of seniors by providing compassionate, medically guided, and personalized care in the comfort of their own homes, upholding dignity, safety, and respect at every stage of aging.

Our Vision

To redefine elder care by making doctor-led, comprehensive home care accessible, reliable, and humane, enabling every senior to age with confidence, comfort, and dignity.

"We believe that aging with grace deserves not just support, but honor—and care that truly feels like home."

Our Services

Quality healthcare right at your door step with well equipped infrastructure.

ICU at Your Home

Bed sore care, I.V lines, DVT prophylaxis, Fluid management, Running infusions, Equipment rental/purchase.

Post Surgical Care

Assisted discharge, Wound & drain care, Pain management, Stitch removal, Vital signs monitoring.

Home Cardiac Care

ECG tracking, Holter monitoring, Post-surgical cardiac recovery, Diet regulation for heart patients.

Home Oncology Service

Chemotherapy at home, Palliative care, Nutrition counselling, Pain management for terminally ill patients.

Specialist Services

Dialysis assistance, Chemoport flush, Home ventilation, Colostomy & ileostomy care, PICC line dressing.

Home Physiotherapy

Neuro & Musculoskeletal recovery, Sports & Chest physiotherapy, High-end portable equipment at home.

Respiratory Care

BIPAP & CPAP at home, Sleep studies, Nebulisation, Oxygen therapy, COPD management.

Everyday Nursing

I.V cannula insertion, Enema application, Ryles tube insertion, Injections, Wound dressing.

Diagnostic Services

Home blood sample collection, Delivery of reports from renowned labs, ECG at home.

Administrative Support

Assistance in paper work, claims, life certificates, banking, and post office works.

Nutritional Services

Individual diet counselling, nutritional assessment, and healthy grocery guidance.

24/7 Emergency Support

Immediate ambulance service and emergency medical intervention at any time of day or night.

Hospitalization Support
