Careers at Digante

Join our compassionate team of caregivers, medical professionals, and support staff dedicated to providing the highest quality of life for our seniors.

Current Openings

We are currently looking for passionate and qualified individuals to join our team in the following roles:

Registered Nurses (ANM/GNM)

Compassionate male and female nursing professionals dedicated to providing round-the-clock medical care and monitoring.

Certified Caregivers & Attendants (GDA)

Dedicated support staff providing essential daily living assistance with empathy and warmth.

Specialized Care Experts

Trained professionals specializing in geriatric care, particularly in managing Alzheimer's, Dementia, Parkinson's, and Schizophrenia.

Therapists & Counselors

Experts in Cognitive, Speech, and Neuro Therapy, alongside skilled Psychological Counselors focused on senior mental health.

Physiotherapists & Massage Specialists

Experienced physical rehabilitation experts aiming to enhance mobility, relieve pain, and improve overall well-being.

Marketing & Sales Professionals

Dynamic individuals to expand our outreach and connect meaningfully with families seeking quality care for their loved ones.

Care & Relationship Managers

Empathetic coordinators ensuring seamless communication between residents, families, and our medical teams.

Medical Equipment Technicians

Skilled technicians to efficiently maintain, troubleshoot, and manage life-support and daily health care equipment.

Professional Dietitians

Experts in geriatric nutrition to curate healthy, balanced, and delicious meals tailored to specific health needs.

Other Healthcare Roles

We are always eager to connect with talented individuals across various healthcare and facility management disciplines.
